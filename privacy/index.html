Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Cifro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data you enter into Cifro — transactions, budgets, notes, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data ever leaves your device.

  • No accounts or sign-up required
  • No internet connection required
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No advertising
  • No backend servers
Because Cifro has no servers and no data transmission, there is nothing to request, delete, or export — your data is already 100% in your control.
